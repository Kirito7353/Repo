A Poem for You

Whispers in the wind, so soft, so light,
Carrying the songs of the quiet night.
Stars above in a velvet sky,
Glisten and twinkle, quietly they sigh.

The world is still, a peaceful rest,
Nature's heartbeat in its very best.
Moments fleeting, passing by,
Like the clouds that drift and fly.

In every breeze, in every tree,
There's a story waiting, wild and free.
So take a moment, close your eyes,
And hear the poetry in the skies.
